# Paper programme completion

## Objective

Produce submission-ready, source-verified manuscripts for the coupling study, SNAP divergence study, and NZ reconciliation findings.

## Requirements

- Build a shared citation ledger from primary sources and authoritative technical documentation.
- Add formal references, methods, reproducibility detail, tables/figures, artifact manifests, limitations, declarations, and reporting checklists.
- Keep all numerical and legal claims machine-auditable against committed artifacts.
- Separate engine agreement, model-scope differences, adapter issues, source-review exceptions, and external adoption claims.
- Prepare journal/arXiv packages but retain `[HUMAN]` submission gates.

## Acceptance

- Each paper passes manuscript/link/citation audits and has a complete submission packet.
- Tables and figures are reproducibly generated from committed data.
- No claim outruns source, fixture, or execution evidence.
- Submission remains deferred until explicit human authorization.
